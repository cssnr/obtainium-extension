Activate the Extension
  • Click the Toolbar Icon.
  • Use the Right-Click Context Menu.
  • Press the Keyboard Shortcut.
Scan QR Code w/ Phone
  1. Open your Phone's Camera Application.
  2. Focus the Camera on the QR Code.
  3. Click Show Options then Open in Browser.

You should be taken directly to the Obtainium Add page.

Activate the Extension
  • Click the 3 dots on the Toolbar.
  • Choose Extensions -> Obtainium Extension.

You should be taken directly to the Obtainium Add page.
